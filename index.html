Mikołaj Bartnicki

sport shooter, guitarist, chess player

The proper Polish pronunciation of my name is roughly Mick-o-why Bartnitzky.

Mikołaj is Polish equivalent of Greek name Nikolaos, meaning victory to the people. There is no short form of Mikołaj in Polish language, but there is a diminutive Mikołajek (pronunced Mick-o-why-eck) which means exactly little Mikołaj.

Family name Bartnicki derives from Polish word bartnik – a honey hunter. Bartnicki means from the honey hunters then. Such surname construction is quite common in Polish language.
