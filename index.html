Mikołaj Bartnicki

engineer, sport shooter, guitarist, chess player

I come from and live in Poland, so I have Polish name. The proper Polish pronunciation of my name is roughly Mick-o-why Bart-nit-ski.

Mikołaj is Polish equivalent of Greek name Nikolaos, meaning victory to the people. There is no short form of Mikołaj in Polish language, but there is a diminutive Mikołajek (pronunced Mick-o-why-eck) which means exactly little Mikołaj.

Family name Bartnicki derives from Polish word bartnik – a honey hunter. Bartnicki means from the honey hunters then. Such construction is quite common in Polish language.
